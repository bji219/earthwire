New elements from TE's design

1
Multi-color segment bar — each sample gets a unique earthy color. Block width = duration ÷ total budget. Empty space shows as diagonal hatching. Colors persist per slot so you can identify samples at a glance.
2
Inline mini waveform — drawn in every filled slot row. A tiny SVG/canvas path showing the shape of the audio. Generated from the decoded AudioBuffer on add. Inverts to white when the slot is selected (dark background).
3
Dark active slot — selected slot flips to black background / white text, matching TE's convention. The expanded waveform below also uses a dark theme.
4
Duration format — shown as MM.SS (e.g. 01.40 = 1.40 seconds, 00.68 = 0.68 seconds). Matches TE exactly.
5
Playmode arrow → — indicates OneShot playback mode per slot. Could be expanded to show Loop (↻) or Gate in future.
6
Colored dot per slot — matches the segment bar color so you can link a slot to its position in the budget bar instantly.
